Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I upgrade my subscription?
A: Go to Billing → Subscription → Upgrade Plan.

Q: How can I change my email?
A: Open Account Settings and update your email address.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Submit a cancellation request from the Billing page.

Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Refunds are processed within 5 business days after approval.

Q: Can I delete my account?
A: Yes. Identity verification is required before account deletion.
